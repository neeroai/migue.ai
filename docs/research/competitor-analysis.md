# Competitor Analysis - WhatsApp AI Assistants

## Overview

**Market**: WhatsApp-based business automation tools
**Geography**: LATAM primary, global secondary
**Segment**: Independent professionals, small businesses

| **Target Market** | Small businesses (5-20 employees) |

### migue.ai Strategy

**Target**: $5-10/month for solo professionals

**Rationale**:
- Lower than Zapia ($29) and Waply ($19+)
- Higher than free (signals quality, sustainable)
- Affordable for LATAM income levels ($500-3000/mo)

**Cost Structure**:
- AI cost: $0.05/user/month (OpenAI GPT-4o-mini)
- Infrastructure: $0.10/user/month (Vercel + Supabase)
- Support: $0.05/user/month (automated + community)
- **Total**: $0.20/user/month

**Margin**: $9.80/user/month at $10 pricing (98% margin)

## Differentiation Strategy

### Primary Differentiators

1. **Spanish-First**: Native LATAM Spanish NLP, not machine translated
2. **WhatsApp-Native**: No app downloads, works where users already are
3. **Voice Support**: Transcription for mobile-first users
4. **Expense Tracking**: Built-in tax compliance support
5. **Simple Pricing**: Flat rate, no per-message fees

### Secondary Differentiators

1. **Multi-provider AI**: OpenAI + Claude fallback (reliability)
2. **Privacy-Focused**: Data in LATAM region, transparent AI usage
3. **Fast Onboarding**: <5 min to first value
4. **24/7 Availability**: Always-on assistant, not business hours only
