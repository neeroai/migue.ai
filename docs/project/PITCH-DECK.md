### A7: Investor FAQ

**Q: Why won't Meta AI crush you?**
A: Meta AI is general-purpose (search, chat). We're productivity-focused (reminders, scheduling, finance). Different use cases. Also, 12-18 month head start in LatAm + enterprise tier (Meta won't prioritize B2B). Our data moat (LatAm workflow insights) compounds with scale.

**Q: What if WhatsApp changes API pricing?**
A: We've modeled 3x price increase and still profitable. Also, WhatsApp wants developer ecosystem (revenue share model unlikely to kill small players). Worst case: migrate to Telegram or SMS (Twilio) in 48 hours (abstracted client layer).

**Q: How defensible is multi-provider AI arbitrage?**
A: Replicating our orchestration layer = 6+ months dev time + ongoing optimization. By then, we have data moat + network effects. Also, new providers emerge (we add them faster than competitors). It's a continuous edge, not one-time advantage.

**Q: Why won't users just use ChatGPT + Google Calendar?**
A: Friction. ChatGPT doesn't execute (just suggests). Calendar requires app switching. migue.ai is ONE place (WhatsApp) with ZERO manual steps. Behavioral: people want convenience more than best-in-class per feature.

**Q: Can you reach $10M ARR on $750K seed?**
A: Conservative math: $750K â†’ 21 months runway â†’ $1.44M ARR by Month 24 â†’ Series A â†’ scale to $10M by Month 48. Bull case (4.5% conversion): $3.6M ARR by Month 36. It's capital efficient, not aspirational.

**Q: What's your unfair advantage?**
A: (1) WhatsApp API expertise (rare skill), (2) Multi-provider AI system (technical moat), (3) LatAm native founders (cultural fluency), (4) Edge-native architecture (performance + cost edge). Combination is hard to replicate.

**Q: What if conversion is only 1.5% (half your assumption)?**
A: Still breaks even by Month 30 (vs Month 21). Series A delayed 6-12 months. Mitigation: A/B test pricing, feature gating, referral incentives. Zapia proves 3%+ is achievable in this market.
